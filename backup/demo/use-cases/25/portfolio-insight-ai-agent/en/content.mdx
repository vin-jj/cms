**Portfolio Insight AI Agent** provides investment teams and clients with a conversational lens on portfolio health. By connecting custodial account data, positions, transactions, benchmarks, and market feeds, the agent answers questions about performance, allocations, and risks—then suggests rebalancing or hedging actions with clear rationale.

BPowered by QueryPie AI’s AI Platform (AIP) and Model Context Protocol (MCP) integrations, the agent aggregates holdings and cash balances, attributes performance across sectors and factors, and monitors drawdowns, VaR, and exposure limits. It can prepare client-ready reports, annotate notable moves, and schedule briefings—posting summaries to email or collaboration tools with full audit trails.

**Key capabilities include:**

- **Performance and attribution**
  - Track time-weighted/dollar-weighted returns; explain contributions by sector, asset, and factor
- **Allocation and exposure**
  - Monitor target vs. actual weights, concentration risks, and geographic/currency exposure
- **Risk and compliance**
  - Watch volatility, drawdowns, VaR, and mandate breaches; generate exceptions and attestations
- **Rebalancing suggestions**
  - Propose trades to restore targets or manage risk; simulate tax impact and transaction costs
- **Client-ready reporting**
  - Produce monthly fact sheets and on-demand briefings with charts and commentary

This use case reduces analysis time, improves decision quality, and enhances client communication. Portfolio managers and advisors gain fast, transparent insights, while compliance and operations retain governance via QueryPie AIP’s permissions, approvals, and auditability.
