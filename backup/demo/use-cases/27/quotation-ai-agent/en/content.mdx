## 🚀 "No matter how complex your quotation is, AIP resolves it in one go."

### 💡 "Can AI really handle calculations this complex?"

In real-world business operations, one of the hardest processes to automate is quotation generation.

It's not just about multiplying unit prices. Once special conditions, surcharges, and layered rules come into play, someone inevitably ends up wrestling with Excel.
It takes time. And at critical moments, human error can creep in.

But with QueryPie AIP, the story changes.

The construction quotation shown in our demo is just one example.
The real point is this: no matter how complex the calculation logic is, AIP transforms it into a structured Skill and executes it flawlessly.

## 🔥 How is this possible?

AIP simplifies complex business logic into a two-step automation framework.

**1️⃣ Just explain it — AIP builds the Skill**

Upload your complex pricing tables and sample quotation files to AIP.
It analyzes the logic and automatically generates a Skill File containing all the calculation rules.

No coding. No complicated configuration.
In just a few clicks, you can download a ready-to-run Skill.

**2️⃣ The Agent executes the Skill and delivers the result**

Attach the generated Skill to an AIP Agent — and you're done.
When a user submits a request, the Agent runs the Skill inside the AIP Sandbox environment.

The result?
An error-free quotation, generated instantly — in your exact format and design.

## 🎯 AIP redefines how quotations are done

This isn't simple text generation.

- ✅ **Unmatched Accuracy** – Even the most complex surcharge logic is calculated 100% based on structured data
- ✅ **Custom Templates Preserved** – Your company's proprietary quotation format remains intact
- ✅ **Unlimited Scalability** – Not just construction quotes, but logistics costs, IT service pricing, hybrid rental fees, and any other complex pricing model

Think your quotation rules are "too complex" to automate?
