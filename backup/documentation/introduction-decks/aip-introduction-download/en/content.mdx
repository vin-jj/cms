## Everything You Need to Know About QueryPie

As AI adoption accelerates, enterprises face the dual challenge of data protection and AI governance. The QueryPie AIP delivers an integrated security platform designed to meet these evolving needs.

Built on proven enterprise technologies, QueryPie AIP connects MCP (Model Context Protocol), AI agents, and LLMs through a unified platform — enabling autonomous AI operations while maintaining strict security compliance.

QueryPie AI offers its own MCP solutions (DAC, SAC, KAC) alongside verified integrations with enterprise platforms such as Slack, GitHub, and AWS. Every AI action is pre-validated, audited, and fully aligned with your organization’s security policies.

Whether for public sector, financial institutions, or cloud-native SaaS businesses, QueryPie provides powerful Zero Trust-based governance combined with flexible automation.
