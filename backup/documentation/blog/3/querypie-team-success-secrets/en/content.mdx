## Increased Efficiency Through Stronger Security

Does security seem difficult? For the QueryPie crew, security isn't a complex or burdensome obstacle, but an essential and reliable "partner" that enhances work efficiency. When security is strong, our crew can focus fully on their tasks without worrying about data breaches or system downtime. Additionally, seamless access to important information and secure processing enable quick collaboration and decision-making.

At QueryPie, we operate automated security processes based on systematic security policies and a robust security environment, minimizing complex procedures and repetitive checks. For example, using the OKTA (IdP) solution with a passwordless approach allows the crew to access various work systems easily and quickly, eliminating the need to remember individual passwords or change them periodically. Additionally, the Kandji (MDM) solution supports fast onboarding, automated device security policy management, management of various work applications, and automated profile deployment for different settings, making it easy and quick to standardize the work environment while maintaining high security standards.

Thanks to this efficient security system, the crew can naturally practice security while staying focused on their core tasks. With the belief that strong security is the path to increased efficiency, we continue to innovate in security.

> - *Leslie, Frontend Team*
> *“With Okta's integrated single sign-on, I can access all of the company's solutions without the hassle of remembering multiple accounts and passwords. I can focus on my work without worrying about security. Additionally, the ability to submit requests anytime, from anywhere, without complicated procedures, and receive a simple completion notification afterward, is incredibly convenient!!”*
> - *Veiner, Backend Team*
> *“Security solutions are crucial for protecting our assets, but they often come with some inconvenience. However, the security processes at QueryPie are designed to minimize these disruptions, making it just as easy to work as it was before we implemented the solutions. In fact, I feel more efficient in certain areas now!”*

## Overflowing Sharing, Quick Decisions, and Fast Execution

QueryPie fosters a culture of self-driven and voluntary participation in work. To support this, we ensure transparency by sharing all relevant information openly. This allows our crew to freely access the information they need to carry out their tasks, helping them clearly understand their roles and objectives, and creating an environment where they can work proactively.

We do not believe there is one right answer to every situation. Instead, we approach challenges in our own unique way, finding the optimal solution for each scenario. To respond swiftly to a rapidly changing environment, we make quick decisions, and once a decision is made, it is immediately put into action. This speed in decision-making and execution strengthens our organization's competitiveness and plays a key role in seizing new opportunities. By sharing the same information and quickly transitioning to new priorities, all crew members were able to adapt flexibly to changes and create outstanding results.

> - *Jane, TPM Team*

## The Growing Tech Culture at QueryPie – DevRel

At QueryPie, our tech organization is built on a culture where developers with diverse experiences and expertise come together to learn and grow. To develop better products, we continuously participate in studies and seminars, share our knowledge through presentations, and improve our skills. Through this exchange, developers gain experience and knowledge from one another, enhancing the team's overall abilities and taking our expertise to the next level.

Moving forward, QueryPie plans to expand its internal tech exchange and reach out to external communities, aiming to grow alongside a broader tech network. We plan to share regular technical content via our blog and exchange insights and experience through various meetups with fellow developers. We hope that QueryPie’s DevRel culture will serve as a platform for growth, connecting with a diverse group of developers.

> - *Mosty, Core Team*

Can you imagine having a vacation as a working professional? At QueryPie, that imagination becomes a reality. QueryPie’s breaks go beyond mere rest — they serve as a chance to gain fresh inspiration. ## Our Commitment to Good Food and Health

At QueryPie, we care deeply about food and health. To create enjoyable lunch breaks and foster comfortable communication among colleagues, we provide lunch subsidies for all crew members. Additionally, we run a "Random Lunch" program each month, encouraging casual conversations across different departments, allowing us to connect beyond work and provide mutual support. We also ensure that energy is always available throughout the day by offering a variety of snacks, drinks, and coffee, creating an environment where our crew can stay energized and focused.

We are equally dedicated to supporting healthy living. Crew members can balance their work and personal life with flexible vacation policies, while we offer various health benefits like health check-ups, group accident insurance, and flu vaccinations. These benefits are designed to help crew members maintain better well-being and stay efficient at work, based on the principles of autonomy and responsibility.

Thanks to these efforts, QueryPie has been recognized as a family-friendly company in 2024 and a youth-friendly small business. Moving forward, we will continue to provide various support to ensure that our crew can work vibrantly while maintaining their physical and mental health.

> - *Thomas, Sales Team*
> *“Random Lunches create opportunities to naturally connect with crew members from different departments who we don’t often get to share meals with. In fact, when collaborating at work, there are times when we don’t fully understand each other’s perspectives. But during a relaxed lunch, we get to better understand each other’s roles, making it a meaningful time.”*
> - *Bill, Human Resources*
> *“This year, I had a health check-up with my parents, and I’m really grateful that they could also benefit from the same health check-up program. The benefits that extend to my family allow me, as well as my parents, to take better care of our health, which brings me peace of mind. The group accident insurance is another great safety net that makes me feel more secure, enabling me to focus better on my work.”*
