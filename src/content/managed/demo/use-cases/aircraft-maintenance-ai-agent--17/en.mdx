**Aircraft Maintenance AI Agent** brings conversational intelligence to aviation MRO operations. By connecting to aircraft health monitoring systems, onboard sensor telemetry, eLogbooks, maintenance manuals (AMM), fault isolation manuals (FIM), service bulletins (SB), and records systems, the agent helps engineers diagnose issues faster, plan tasks, and document work with audit-ready accuracy.

Built on QueryPie AI’s AI Platform (AIP) with Model Context Protocol (MCP) integrations, the agent correlates fault codes, flight-phase events, and maintenance history to suggest likely root causes and recommended procedures. It can retrieve relevant AMM/FIM steps, generate checklists, verify parts availability, and open work packages in MRO systems—posting summaries to Teams/Slack and capturing approvals where required.

**Key capabilities include:**

- **Diagnostic reasoning**
  - Map fault codes and sensor anomalies to probable causes; propose AMM/FIM steps and required tooling
- **Knowledge retrieval**
  - Pull precise procedures from manuals and bulletins; link to effectivity and revision levels
- **Workflow orchestration**
  - Create work orders, route approvals, and update maintenance records with traceable actions
- **Compliance support**
  - Enforce sign-offs, record time and materials, and generate audit-ready documentation
- **Fleet and reliability analytics**
  - Surface repeat defects, deferred maintenance trends, and component reliability signals

This use case reduces mean time to diagnose (MTTD) and mean time to repair (MTTR), increases aircraft availability, and standardizes documentation to meet regulatory obligations. Engineers and planners gain a single conversational interface that combines diagnostics, knowledge, and workflow—while safety and compliance teams retain full oversight through QueryPie AIP’s centralized governance and audit trails.
