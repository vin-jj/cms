**Investment Analyst AI Agent** augments buy-side and sell-side research by automating the workflows that slow analysts down. Through natural language, users can run screens, pull filings and transcripts, summarize earnings, refresh models, and draft coverage notes—without juggling terminals and spreadsheets.

Running on QueryPie’s AI Platform (AIP) with Model Context Protocol (MCP) integrations, the agent connects to market data feeds, fundamentals databases, news, transcripts, and document repositories. It can extract KPIs from 10-K/10-Qs, update assumptions, run scenario analysis, and produce outputs tailored to your process: dashboards, model diffs, investment memos, or client notes. Approvals, permissions, and audit logs are enforced end-to-end.

**Key capabilities include:**

- **Idea generation and screening**
  - Create custom screens by sector/factors; flag anomalies and momentum/valuation outliers
- **Earnings and news summarization**
  - Summarize transcripts, filings, and press releases; extract guidance changes and KPI deltas
- **Modeling and scenarios**
  - Update drivers, run bull/base/bear scenarios, and compare valuation frameworks
- **Portfolio impact**
  - Estimate P&L sensitivity and risk exposure; suggest trades or hedges with rationale
- **Reporting and artifacts**
  - Produce coverage updates, client notes, and dashboards with charts and references

This use case raises research velocity and consistency while keeping compliance in the loop. Analysts get faster workflows and clearer deliverables, PMs get decision-ready summaries, and compliance retains control through QueryPie AIP’s policy enforcement and full auditability.
