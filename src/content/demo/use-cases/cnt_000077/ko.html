Reporting & Incident Management AI Agent centralizes operational awareness by ingesting unit reports, readiness metrics, logistics data, and incident feeds. Command staff and watch officers can ask natural language questions to pull status across units, generate standardized SITREPs, and trigger alerts or tasking based on thresholds and incident categories.

Built on QueryPie AI’s AI Platform (AIP) with Model Context Protocol (MCP) integrations, the agent connects to readiness dashboards, incident management tools, logistics and personnel systems, and comms platforms. It correlates events across domains—operations, intelligence, logistics, medical—creating a clear operational picture with timestamps, geolocation, and unit identifiers. The agent supports approval-based workflows for notifications and orders, maintaining a complete audit trail.

Key capabilities include:

  • Status aggregation and SITREPs

  • Compile unit status, equipment availability, logistics levels, and personnel readiness into standardized reports

  • Threshold alerts and incident triage

  • Detect threshold breaches, classify incidents, and propose response playbooks

  • Tasking and coordination

  • Create and assign tasks, track acknowledgments, and update situation boards

  • Geospatial context

  • Map events and units; filter by area of operations and time windows

  • Knowledge retrieval

  • Retrieve SOPs, ROEs, and playbooks; generate checklists for incident response

This use case reduces reporting overhead, improves timeliness and accuracy, and strengthens command and control. Leaders gain a conversational, auditable interface for situational awareness and response, while policies and approvals are enforced through QueryPie AIP’s governance.
