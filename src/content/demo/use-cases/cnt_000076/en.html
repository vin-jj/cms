Military Service & HR AI Agent brings conversational automation to defense HR operations. It unifies soldier records, duty assignments, qualifications, training, leaves, and evaluations into a single, secure interface. Units can query personnel status, generate orders, update rosters, process leave requests, and produce reports without navigating multiple systems.

Running on QueryPie AI’s AI Platform (AIP) with Model Context Protocol (MCP) integrations, the agent connects to personnel databases, training systems, readiness dashboards, and document repositories. Command staff can request roster changes, check MOS/AFSC qualifications and training currency, validate deployment eligibility, and issue admin documents with auditable approvals. The agent enforces policies for rank, billet requirements, rotation cycles, and duty/rest constraints while maintaining a full audit trail.

Key capabilities include:

  • Personnel status and readiness

  • Retrieve soldier/airman profiles, qualifications, medical/deployability flags, and training currency

  • Assignment and roster management

  • Generate and update unit rosters; match billets to rank/MOS/AFSC; manage rotations and temporary duty (TDY)

  • Leave and pass administration

  • Process leave requests, validate eligibility, route approvals, and update calendars and duty logs

  • Evaluations and records

  • Draft performance evaluations, track due items, update awards, and maintain service records

  • Orders and documentation

  • Create assignment orders, TDY orders, memos, and attendance rosters with template-driven automation

This use case reduces administrative workload, accelerates decision-making, and improves data accuracy across units. Leaders gain a conversational interface for HR operations, while compliance, security, and oversight are preserved via QueryPie AIP’s centralized permissioning, approval workflows, and comprehensive auditing.
