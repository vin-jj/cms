Achieving device security through MDM: Kandji x QueryPie

In this webinar, our security experts Brant Hwang (CEO, QueryPie) and Kenny Park (CISO, QueryPie) discuss how to secure your Apple devices and utilize Kandji’s powerful, purpose-built tools to create an intuitive, streamlined experience for admins and users alike.
